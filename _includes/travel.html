Travel

✈️ Airport ✈️

There are a couple of options for guests that will be flying to our wedding. The closest and most convenient airport to fly into is Madison (MSN). Most of our hotel blocks provide a free shuttle to and from the MSN airport. Please inquire when booking. Otherwise, it is a quick Uber ride from the airport to the hotel. Guests can also fly into Milwaukee (MKE) or Chicago (ORD) and rent a car and drive. The drive from MKE to Madison is about 1 hour 30 minutes and the drive from ORD to Madison is about 2 hours 30 minutes.

For guests staying in the downtown Madison hotels, shuttle transportation will be provided between the hotel and wedding venue on the day of the wedding.
