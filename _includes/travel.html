Travel

✈️ Airport ✈️

There are a couple of options for guests that will be flying to our wedding. The closest and most convenient airport to fly into is Madison (MSN). Guests can also fly into Milwaukee (MKE) or Chicago (ORD) and rent a car and drive.

For guests staying in the downtown Madison hotels, shuttle transportation will be provided between the hotel and wedding venue on the day of the wedding.

MSN

Fly straight into Madison. Most of our hotel blocks provide a free shuttle to and from the MSN airport. Please inquire when booking. Otherwise, it is a quick Uber ride from the airport to the hotel.

MKE

Fly into Milwaukee and the drive time is approximately 1 hour 30 minutes.

ORD

Fly into Chicago and the drive time is approximately 2 hours 30 minutes.
